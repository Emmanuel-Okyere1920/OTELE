Graphic Design

the art or skill of combining text and pictures in advertisements, magazines, or books.

Graphic Design for starters

According to the American Institute of Graphic Arts (AIGA), graphic design is defined as
“the art and practice of planning and projecting ideas and experiences with visual and textual content.”
In other terms, graphic design communicates certain ideas or messages in a visual way.
